# Inti

Official public overview of the Inti ecosystem.

This repository exists to provide a clear, timestamped public record of Inti brand usage, product naming, and high-level direction.

## Mission

Inti builds practical human+AI systems for thinking, creating, and collaborating.

## Product Family (High-Level)

- **Inti Docs** — AI-assisted document understanding and knowledge workflows.
- **Inti Docs Enterprise** — organizational and team-focused deployment of Inti Docs.
- **Inti Reader** — read/listen/discuss experiences for deep comprehension.
- **Inti Author** — human+AI co-writing and structured creation workflows.
- **Inti Creator** — artistic human+AI collaborative creation.
- **Inti Code** — software development support and engineering workflows.
- **IntiNet** — long-term network layer for connected Inti systems.
- **Intellipedia** — structured knowledge layer within the broader Inti ecosystem.

> Note: Product scope, names, and sequencing may evolve.

## Official Properties

- Primary website: **https://myinti.ai**
- Current known subdomains in use/planned include product-specific paths under `*.myinti.ai`.

## Why this repo exists

1. Publish a canonical high-level description of Inti.
2. Establish a public history of naming and brand continuity.
3. Provide lightweight media/brand references for community and partners.
