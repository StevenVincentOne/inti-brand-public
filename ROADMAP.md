# Inti Roadmap (High-Level, Non-Binding)

This roadmap communicates direction, not guarantees.

## Near Term

- Public launch and iteration of **Inti Docs**
- Core user feedback loop and product hardening
- Brand and documentation standardization

## Mid Term

- Expansion toward **Inti Reader** and **Inti Author** workflows
- Initial rollout path for **Inti Docs Enterprise**
- Deeper knowledge structuring patterns tied to the Inti ecosystem

## Long Term

- Broader ecosystem capabilities including **Inti Code**, **Inti Creator**, and **IntiNet**
- Evolution of connected, user-centered intelligence infrastructure
- Growth of **Intellipedia** as a structured knowledge layer

---

All dates, sequencing, and feature scope are subject to change.
