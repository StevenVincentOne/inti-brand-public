# Inti — Press Factsheet (v1)

## What is Inti?

Inti is a human+AI ecosystem focused on helping people and teams think, create, and collaborate through practical AI-native workflows.

## Core Focus Areas

- Documents and knowledge workflows
- Reading, comprehension, and discussion
- Human+AI co-creation
- Long-term infrastructure for connected intelligence systems

## Product Line (Current Public Names)

- Intellipedia
